About Department

The chemistry department at present bears no specialized laboratory. Only laboratory facilities required for practical sessions of first year degree & diploma students are available. The laboratory is spacious & is capable to accommodate degree & diploma batches simultaneously. At a single session about 70 students can perform practical. This laboratory is thus general chemistry laboratory.

The details are as below:

Name of laboratory General Chemistry laboratory
Laboratory area 2339.92 Sq.ft.
Maximum batches accommodated 03 batches of 22 students each.
Weekly Occupancy 20 Hrs (Degree) 20 Hrs (Diploma)
